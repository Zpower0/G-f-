Resume Writing Tips

Learn from these examples and improve your own resume

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers and metrics to show impact. Instead of "managed social media," write "Increased engagement by 150%."

Target Your Resume

Customize for each job application. Use keywords from the job description that ATS systems look for.

Clear Structure

Use consistent formatting with clear sections. Recruiters spend only 6-7 seconds on initial scan.

Action Verbs

Start bullet points with strong action verbs: Led, Developed, Implemented, Increased, Reduced, etc.
